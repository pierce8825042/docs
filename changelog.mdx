<Update label="July 1, 2026" tags={["Feature","Contacts"]} description="Streamlined contact import" rss={{ title:"Streamlined contact import",description:"Import contacts in fewer steps with smart field mapping, inline custom fields, and an import preview." }}>
  ### What changed

  Importing contacts now moves you straight from upload or paste into a mapping review screen. Common fields are matched automatically, empty columns are skipped, and your previous mappings are reused. You can create custom fields inline without restarting, review a preview showing duplicate and invalid counts, and finish with a single **Import contacts** action.

  ### Why it matters

  Fewer clicks and clearer feedback make onboarding contact lists faster and reduce mistakes before you commit an import.

  ### How to use

  Open **Contacts → Import Contacts**, upload or paste your CSV or XLSX list, review the suggested mappings and the import preview, adjust or create custom fields inline if needed, then select **Import contacts**.

  ### Who it affects

  Admins and onboarding users who import contact lists.

  {/* changelog-pr:784 */}
</Update>

<Update label="June 30, 2026" tags={["Feature","Admin"]} description="Edit your profile name and email" rss={{ title:"Edit your profile name and email",description:"Update your first and last name and request an email address change from Settings." }}>
  ### What changed

  You can now review and update your first and last name from **Settings → Your Profile**, and request a change to the email address you sign in with.

  ### Why it matters

  Keep your account identity current without contacting an admin. Your name updates flow through to the app sidebar and shared screens automatically.

  ### How to use

  Open **Settings → Your Profile**. Your current first and last name appear above the edit controls — update either field and save. To change your sign-in email, enter the new address and select **Send Confirmation Email**, then confirm from the message sent to the new address. Your existing email keeps working until you confirm.

  ### Who it affects

  All users managing their own account profile.

  {/* changelog-pr:758 */}
</Update>

<Update label="June 27, 2026" tags={["Feature","Campaigns"]} description="Campaign completion reports" rss={{ title:"Campaign completion reports",description:"Email a PDF summary automatically when a campaign finishes." }}>
  ### What changed

  When a campaign finishes, Amplifi can email a PDF summary to the address you set on the compose screen.

  ### Why it matters

  You get a shareable record of delivery results without exporting from the app.

  ### How to use

  On the campaign compose step, enter an optional **Completion report email**. After the campaign reaches **Completed**, the report is generated and sent automatically.

  ### Who it affects

  Campaign managers and admins who run bulk sends.
</Update>

<Update label="June 27, 2026" tags={["Feature","Compliance"]} description="Shared campaign opt-out" rss={{ title:"Shared campaign opt-out",description:"Contacts can opt out from a shared campaign link without signing in." }}>
  ### What changed

  Contacts viewing a shared campaign thread can opt out without signing in.

  ### Why it matters

  Recipients can stop messages quickly, which helps compliance and reduces support load.

  ### How to use

  Open the shared campaign link, select a contact thread, and use **Opt out** on that conversation.

  ### Who it affects

  Anyone with a shared campaign link; org admins who publish shared pages.
</Update>

<Update label="June 27, 2026" tags={["Improvement","Inbox"]} description="Live inbox updates" rss={{ title:"Live inbox updates",description:"Conversation lists and threads update in near real time while you work." }}>
  ### What changed

  Conversation lists and message threads update in near real time while you work in the app.

  ### Why it matters

  You see replies and status changes without manually refreshing the page.
